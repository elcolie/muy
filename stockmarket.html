Question

Stock Market

The given file sp500.txt has the date and closing price of the S&P500 stock market index for each trading day from 1927 until today.

The format is:

<date> <closing price> ^-- column 1 ^-- column 15

Imagine you had invested and then waited for 60 trading days. Calculate the best and worst dates to buy in / sell out (measured by percentage return) for a 60-trading day period.

Example, the return from 1980-01-15 to 1980-04-10:

1980-01-15 111.139999 1980-04-10 104.080002

( 104.080002 / 111.139999 ) - 1

=> -0.06352345747276833 or -6.35% (ie, negative return, a bad 60 days)
